Wings of fire

APJ ABDUL KALAM

"Wings of Fire" is an inspiring autobiography of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the former President of India and a renowed scientist. The book chronicles his early life in Tamil Nadu, his journey in the Indian space and missile programs, and his vision for India's future. Through personal anecdotes and reflections, Kalam shares his struggles, achievements, and the values that guided him. Emphasizing hard work, perseverance, and the power of dreams, "Wings of Fire" serves as a Motivational guide for readers, encouraging them to pursue their aspirations and contribute to society.
